Welcome to Stroke of Insight Massage

Stroke of Insight Massage is small business that provides customized massage therapy to individuals looking for therapeutic, empathetic, professional touch. People enjoy our service because our goal is to connect with you, hear your needs, concerns & goals, and create a massage therapy experience based on this conversation.

Brief History

By John Smith posted July 20, 2018

After graduating from massage school, I have had a massage about once/week. I’ve been to massage therapy businesses and worked with self employed therapists as well. The biggest insight I’ve had is that the best massages have been when the therapist has a mindful, kind and healing touch. The pressure could be light to heavy to anywhere in between. It was the intention behind the touch and the human connection that made the difference for me.

Here is what I want For You

A customized massage that suits your needs and addresses your concerns.
You feel listened to and heard.
Clarity on the goal, areas of focus, areas to avoid, pain, desired level of pressure, and any preferences for massage styles
Your massage may include elements of Swedish, Deep Tissue, Myofascial Release, Trigger Point Release, Stretching, Reflexology, Acupressure, Hand, Foot and/or Scalp Massage
If you do not know what these different massage styles are, no worries - we will happily customize based on our conversation.
An experience that is therapeutic and delightful!

About

She works at a local hospital as an Occupational Therapist in addition to her Massage Practice.
